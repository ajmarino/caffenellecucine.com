Caffe Nelle Cucine offers some of the best fresh baked pastries in Warren County. Baked here on premise, we take care in using only the best ingredients in our desserts and baked goods. Our full time professional baker takes the time to hand craft beautiful pastries for our shop. We offer many different items on any given day ranging from Italian desserts, to country pies, to New York style pastries. Stop in today to check out our full assortment, as well as talk to our baker about any special orders you may have. We accept orders for special occasions such as birthday parties, weddings, communions, or any other get together. Contact our baker Sam at sam@caffenellecucine.com.
